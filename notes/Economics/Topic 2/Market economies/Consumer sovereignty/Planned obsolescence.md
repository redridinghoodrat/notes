- Most consumers prefer to have the highest level of technology or the latest form of technology. 
- Businesses are very aware of this and, to increase profitability, produce goods that are designed to wear out or are superceded by new technology, forcing them to make more purchases from the company
- [[Marketing]] also encourages the consumer to keep up with fads (short-lived trends or behaviours that gain rapid popularity bud face quickly, for example: fashion)
- Planned obsolescence is a form of [[Anti-competitive behavior|anti-competitive behavior]]
